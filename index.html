Our Story

Born From a
Love of Fragrance

LUMIÈRE was founded in Paris in 1998 by master perfumer Éloise Dumont, with a simple belief: every person deserves a scent that feels like a second skin.

Our perfumers travel the world sourcing the finest ingredients — Bulgarian roses, Indian oud, Sicilian bergamot — blending them into timeless compositions that evolve beautifully on your skin.
